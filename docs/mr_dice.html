Mr. Dice


Mr. Dice is a 2.5D platformer where you play as Mr. Dice, who finds himself lost in the house's mess and wants to reunite with the other game pieces.

Game made with Unity for the GMTK Game Jam 2022 with theme: Roll of the Dice.

My contributions to this project are:

  • Building of all the levels, main menu and pause menu.
  • One way platforms.
  • Transition system.
  • Collectibles system and its particles.
  • Bugs fixing.
  • Overall help when needed.
