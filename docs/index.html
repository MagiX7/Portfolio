About me

Greetings, my name is David Jesús González López and I am currently in the latest year of Videogames Design and Development Bachelor's Degree at CITM-UPC.
Despite I have two names, most people only use the first one, but you can use the one you like the most. Or both!

During my journey at the college I have done a lot of projects, either personal or for the Bachelor. This helped me to know that I really like the graphics aspect of making games, such as the rendering pipeline or the rendering architecture. But overall, what I enjoy the most is shaders programming.

On the other hand, I also grew up on my communication skills, time management, problem solving capability and projects organization thanks to all the games I have made with other colleagues during this past years.

And last, but not least, I think game developers should always recycle themselves and adapt to the new born technologies. This is why I always try to learn something new, like new graphics APIs or different shading languages.
