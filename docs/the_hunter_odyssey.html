The Hunter Odyssey


Hunter Odyssey is a game where you find yourself submerged in a world of fantasy, with dangerous enemies and unique puzzles.

During your journey you will need to pull out your bounty hunter skills to rescue the prince from the most important region of the country, who is supposedly kidnapped by a majestuous monster and get the reward.

This project was done from scratch using C++ and SDL where I took the role of lead in a group of seven people in order to help and motivate them to share their ideas and come up with one that everyone likes.

My contributions to this project are:

  • Meetings recording.
  • Creation and maintentance of Github Projects (classic).
  • Gamepad input system.
  • EASTL implementation.
  • Logo screen.
  • Optimizations and remove memeory leaks.
  • Dialogue system alongisde other three people and writing machine effect.
  • Inventory system alongside other three people.
  • Helped with the overall feedback.
