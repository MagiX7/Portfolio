Super Space


Super Space is a dystopian version of one of the best-known games on the VR platform, born from a university project of 4 young students, in which you can have fun shooting enemies on different levels and using your skills creatively in battle !! Made for short and enjoyable games, it is an ideal casual game to play alone or with your friends and compete for the best score.

Game made with Unity and presented on the Indie Dev Day 2022.

My contributions to this project are:

  • Portals behaviour and its animation.
  • Meshes dissolve effect.
  • Feedback provider.
  • Testing.
  • Overall help when needed.
